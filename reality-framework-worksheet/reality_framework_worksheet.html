REALITY FRAMEWORK

A systematic approach to building your authentic brand and sustainable business

Work through each phase sequentially to transform your identity, expertise, and vision into a thriving reality. Check off each milestone as you complete it.
R

RESONANCE

Identity Ignition

Goal
Achieve soul-level clarity and define your authentic internal frequency
This is where you get past imposter syndrome and discover who you really are. You'll dig deep into your core identity, map your personal history, and create a clear vision of your authentic self. Think of this as building your internal compass—the foundation everything else builds on.
Complete When You Have:
Written your personal story in a way that emotionally captivates and have a system for updating it weekly
Documented a clear vision of who you are now, who you want to become, and what's blocking you
Defined your core identity and personal mission statement that feels authentic to you
E

EXPERTISE

Embodied Brand

Goal
Extract your embodied mastery and encode it into a powerful, recognizable signal
Here you identify what you're undeniably great at and turn it into your brand foundation. This isn't about what you studied or wish you were good at—it's about your lived experience and proven skills. You'll create frameworks and proof points that show your unique value.
Complete When You Have:
Identified 3-5 core areas where your experience, skills, and personal story naturally overlap
Created a unique method, process, or framework that only you could have developed
Gathered concrete examples and case studies that prove your ability to create transformation
A

ALIGNMENT

Audience Fit

Goal
Sync your authentic signal to the felt needs of your ideal audience
Now you test your identity and expertise in the real world to see what resonates. You'll actively engage with your audience, learn their language, and discover what content creates genuine connection. This is about finding the overlap between who you are and what people actually need.
Complete When You Have:
Completed a focused engagement sprint posting daily and commenting extensively to gather real feedback
Documented the exact words, phrases, and emotional triggers your audience responds to
Created a set of content themes that consistently generate engagement, saves, and meaningful conversations
L

LAUNCH

Offer or App

Goal
Manifest your reality as something others can experience, pay for, or join
This is where your identity and expertise become a real product or service in the world. You'll create something that embodies your unique approach and allows people to experience transformation through working with you. This could be coaching, a course, software, or any other format that fits your vision.
Complete When You Have:
Built and launched your core offer that authentically represents your identity and solves real problems
Created a story-driven journey that makes your offer feel meaningful rather than transactional
Established a clear way for interested people to say yes and get started with you
I

ITERATION

Feedback Alchemy

Goal
Transform friction, confusion, and resistance into upgraded clarity and better systems
Once your offer is live, you'll gather real feedback from real users and systematically improve based on what you learn. This phase is about turning problems into improvements and building a habit of continuous refinement based on actual data rather than assumptions.
Complete When You Have:
Established a regular system for collecting honest feedback from your audience and customers
Identified and categorized the key gaps in your current approach and prioritized them by impact
Created a regular schedule for making improvements based on what you're learning
T

TESTING

Proof of Reality

Goal
Systematically validate your system under real-world pressure
Now you stress-test everything you've built. Can your identity, message, and offer hold up under pressure? Do people actually pay? Do they get results? This phase is about proving your reality works not just in theory, but when it matters most.
Complete When You Have:
Built a system to track what's actually working in terms of conversions, engagement, and results
Put your offer through real-world pressure tests and learned where it succeeds or breaks down
Conducted a thorough review of what's working emotionally, functionally, and financially
Y

YIELD

Sustainable Growth

Goal
Build momentum engines that regenerate and scale your reality over time
The final phase focuses on creating systems that grow and sustain themselves. You'll build repeatable processes that generate momentum, revenue, and community without burning you out. This is about creating a business that energizes you rather than depletes you.
Complete When You Have:
Created a repeating cycle that continuously brings in new audience members and converts them
Built multiple ways for people to engage with you at different levels and price points
Assembled a collection of proven content and systems that compound your results over time
